💡 The meaning of the formula:

It represents the mathematical law of accumulation through compounding: qn represents the total growth factor after n steps. It tells you how many times larger the last "potential" term has become compared to the start. This is why geometric sums grow so explosively - the n is in the exponent.

Thanks to this formula, you can calculate the total result of a repetitive multiplying process without having to go through every single step.
